z o ł z o

120,000 men were recognized in Poland as violence victims during 2005-2016. Although this number is much lower than in the case of victims of the opposite sex, it should not be underestimated. Women as often as men use many forms of oppression, but it is frequently difficult to prove these behaviors, especially in case of psychological abuse. Zołzo is a set designed for quiet revenge which is intended to draw attention to the fact that a woman, as well as a man, can be an oppressor. The set consists of redesigned items that are very likely to be found in women’s bags. Theese are: a spikey curler for breaking cables and tearing clothes, a mascara spike suited for scratching his car or a screen of his phone, a bottle of acidic scent for destroing the wardrobe of your revenge object, a scented dropper consisting water from an old ashtray to spray near your victim and a key file which can permamently damage all the most important keys. ZOŁZO is a subtle and discreet set of tools designed for quiet but significant revenge on an object that once showed up for a dinner too late, lied too many times and complained a lot more when you had a good day. Finally, you have the chance to take control over your life and let him know that you are not a woman whose freedom or expression can be limited. Be independent. Be ZOŁZO.
